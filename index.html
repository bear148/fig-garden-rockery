Welcome to Fig Garden Rockery!

Fig Garden Rockery was born from the need for an outlet for rock, sand, aggregate, and humus, open to the public, at an affordable price. We are located on Blythe, near Shaw and Golden State and are open Monday through Saturday for your project needs. We are a retail and wholesale outlet.
